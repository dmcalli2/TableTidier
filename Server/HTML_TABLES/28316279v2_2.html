Supplemental Figure S1. Subgroup analyses of the primary efficacy outcome for rivaroxaban 20 mg vs. aspirin 100 mg. HR denotes hazard ratio; CI confidence interval; DVT deep vein thrombosis; PE pulmonary embolism. Primary efficacy outcome: composite of fatal and non-fatal venous thromboembolism including unexplained death where PE could not be ruled out. Definition extent of index DVT or PE: 1) limited, if thrombosis was not above the popliteal vein or PE was confined to a single lobe involving ≤25% of the vasculature of that lobe, or 2) extensive, if thrombosis involved the common femoral and/or iliac vein or if involved multiple lobes and affected >25% of the entire pulmonary vasculature, or 3) intermediate, all other results for thrombosis and PE. Fragile: age > 75 or weight ≤ 50 kg or creatinine clearance < 50 ml/min
