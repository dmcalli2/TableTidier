Secondary end points included remission at week 6 and at both weeks 6 and 26 in patients with a baseline serum CRP level of at least 10 mg per liter and a decrease of at least 100 points in the CDAI score and remission at week 6 and at both weeks 6 and 26 among all patients, regardless ofthe CRP level. Post hoc exploratory analyses were conducted on the use of immunosuppressive agents, concomitant glucocorticoid therapy, and previous treatment with infliximab.
