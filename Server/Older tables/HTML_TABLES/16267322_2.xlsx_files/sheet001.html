Figure 2 (facing page). Percentage of Patients in the ENACT-1 Trial with a Clinical Response at Each Visit (Panel A), in Remission at Each Visit (Panel B), with a Response at Week 10 (Panel C), and in Remission at Week 10 (Panel D).
A response was defined as a decrease from baseline in the CDAI score of at least 70 points. Remission was de-fined as a decrease in the CDAI score to less than 150 points. Significant differences between the treatment groups are shown.
