Figure S2: Effect of rosuvastatin on postoperative Holter monitor AF, overall and by subgroups defined retrospectively
* Post-randomization characteristic Odds ratios comparing the incidence of AF after cardiac surgery (assessed by 5-day Holter ECG monitoring) among patients allocated rosuvastatin and those allocated placebo. For each subgroup, odds ratios are plotted as squares, with the size of each square proportional to the amount of statistical information, and horizontal lines represent 95% confidence intervals (CI).
For all patients, the odds ratio and 95% CI are represented by a diamond, with values and statistical significance test given alongside. Squares or diamonds to the left of the vertical line indicate benefit with rosuvastatin, but the benefit is significant only if the horizontal line or diamond does not overlap the vertical line. Participants with a missing value for a particular subgroup are not shown (but do contribute to the overall diamond).
