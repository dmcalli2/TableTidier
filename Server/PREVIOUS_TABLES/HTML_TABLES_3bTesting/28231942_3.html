Baseline values for low-density lipoprotein cholesterol (LDL-C), total cholesterol, high-density lipoprotein cholesterol (HDL-C), triglycerides (TG) and non-HDL-C were determined at the time of the qualifying ACS event. Baseline values for high-sensitivity C-reactive protein (hs-CRP) and apolipoprotein-B (Apo-B) were determined at the time of randomization. P-value<0.05 for 1 year achieved values for comparison of simva vs EZ/simva for overall population and within risk category for all measured values. To convert the values for cholesterol to millimoles per liter, multiply by 0.02586.
