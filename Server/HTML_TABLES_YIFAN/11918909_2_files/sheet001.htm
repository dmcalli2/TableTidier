We analysed the primary endpoint in several  prespecified subgroups. Among patients with no history of  previous myocardial infarction, the median salvage index  was 0-64 (IQR 0-38-0-83) in the stent groups and 0-44  (0，16-0-69) in the fibrinolysis group (p=0-009); among  those presenting at hospital within 6 h of symptom onset,  it was 0-62 (0-37-0-83) in the stent group and 0-42  (0^13-0^65) in the fibrinolysis group (p=0-003); and  among those who did not undergo an unplanned  revascularisation procedure before follow-up scintigraphy  (the population after exclusion of the five patients in the  fibrinolysis group who underwent rescue stenting), the  salvage index was 0-60 (0-37-0-82) in the stent group and  0.41 (0，13-0，58) in the fibrinolysis group (p=0-0009).
