Data are adjusted proportion (95% CI), using logistic regression analysis with treatment and strata as factors. aJBS-2 deﬁnition of secondary prevention for patients with existing cardiovascular disease (CVD) or CVD and diabetes; primary prevention for high-risk patients or patients with diabetes but no CVD. bNICE deﬁnition of secondary prevention for patients with existing CVD and ⁄ or diabetes; primary prevention for high-risk patients without diabetes.
